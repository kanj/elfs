14.3. Copy to Target

We just created a cleaned-up version of our build, now compress it into an initial ramdisk so it can be archived and transferred to the target. 


cd ${CLFS}/build/binclock

( find . | cpio -o --format=newc | gzip ) > ../binclock.gz

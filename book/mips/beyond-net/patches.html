14.3. Beyond CLFS Networking Patches

In addition to the packages, several patches are also required. These patches correct any mistakes in the packages that should be fixed by the maintainer. The patches also make small modifications to make the packages easier to work with. The following patches will be needed in order to build the associated packages:

IPTables Fixes Patch - 5 KB:

Download: http://patches.cross-lfs.org/embedded-dev/iptables-1.4.2-fixes-1.patch

MD5 sum: 5a8bc894706019ea4f42e506633a49bf

Libnl Fixes Patch - 3 KB:

Download: http://patches.cross-lfs.org/embedded-dev/libnl-git-fixes-1.patch

MD5 sum: 773b0d979f1592f757dd30d47ae179fa

Total size of these patches: about 8 KB

11.3. Needed Patches

In addition to the bootloader packages, several patches are also required. These patches correct any mistakes in the packages that should be fixed by the maintainer. The patches also make small modifications to make the packages easier to work with. The following patches will be needed to build a CLFS system:

Colo Make Patch - 2 KB:

Download: ../patches/colo-1.22-make_fix-1.patch

MD5 sum: b89575a0e385b5366b19a6b5f176d49b

Colo Relocation Patch - .7 KB:

Download: ../patches/colo-1.22-relocation_fix-1.patch

MD5 sum: e0607ee1071f2f805ffa1ef1c5b1a766

Total size of these patches: about 2.7 KB
